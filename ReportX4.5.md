## ICT Framework Analysis

### Core ICT Concepts Implemented

#### 1. Buy-Side and Sell-Side Liquidity

**Buy-Side Liquidity (Demand Zones):**
- Areas where large clusters of buy stop losses reside (below current price)
- Represented by previous swing lows and consolidation areas
- Smart money targets these zones to fill large sell positions
- Identified by pivot points with volume confirmation

**Sell-Side Liquidity (Supply Zones):**
- Areas where large clusters of sell stop losses reside (above current price)
- Represented by previous swing highs and consolidation areas
- Smart money targets these zones to fill large buy positions
- Higher probability when multiple touches with increasing volume

#### 2. Order Flow Trading

**Order Flow Metrics:**
- **Buying Pressure**: Aggressive buying when close is near high with strong volume
- **Selling Pressure**: Aggressive selling when close is near low with strong volume
- **Delta**: Net difference between buying and selling pressure
- **Order Flow Direction**: bullish, bearish, or neutral based on delta

**Order Blocks:**
- **Bullish Order Block**: Strong down candle before strong up move (institutional footprint)
- **Bearish Order Block**: Strong up candle before strong down move
- Validated by continuation after consolidation
- Strength based on body/range ratio and volume spike

#### 3. Kill Zones (Time-Based Analysis)

| Kill Zone | Time (EST) | Characteristics | Volatility Multiplier |
|-----------|------------|-----------------|----------------------|
| Asian Session | 5pm-12am | Accumulation/Distribution | 0.6x |
| London Open KZ | 2am-5am | European liquidity enters | 1.2x |
| London Session | 3am-11am | Trend development | 1.0x |
| New York Open KZ | 8am-11am | Highest volatility | 1.5x |
| New York Session | 8am-4pm | US market hours | 1.3x |
| London Close KZ | 10am-12pm | Profit taking | 0.9x |

#### 4. Market Structure Shift (MSS)

**Bullish MSS:**
- Higher high and higher low detected
- Indicates trend reversal to upside
- Confirmation with volume increase

**Bearish MSS:**
- Lower high and lower low detected
- Indicates trend reversal to downside
- Confirmation with volume increase

#### 5. Premium and Discount Zones

**Premium Zone (above 70% of range):**
- Price is high, look for sell setups
- Target sell-side liquidity zones

**Discount Zone (below 30% of range):**
- Price is low, look for buy setups
- Target buy-side liquidity zones

**Fair Value (30%-70% of range):**
- Neutral zone, wait for confirmation
- No edge, avoid trading

## Level 2 Market Data Analysis

### Understanding Level 2 Data

**True Level 2 Data (DOM - Depth of Market):**
- Shows all pending buy and sell orders at different price levels
- Reveals institutional footprints and large limit orders
- Provides real-time order book imbalance
- Identifies absorption and exhaustion patterns

**Our Proxy Approach:**
Since true Level 2 DOM data is not available via standard market data feeds, we derive proxy metrics from OHLCV price action patterns.

### Level 2 Proxy Metrics

#### 1. Order Book Imbalance

**Bid Volume Proxy**: Buying pressure from strong up candles
- Close near high with strong volume = aggressive buying
- Measures institutional demand

**Ask Volume Proxy**: Selling pressure from strong down candles
- Close near low with strong volume = aggressive selling
- Measures institutional supply

**Imbalance Ratio**: Bid Volume / Ask Volume
- Ratio > 1.5: Strong buying pressure
- Ratio < 0.67: Strong selling pressure
- Ratio ≈ 1.0: Balanced order flow

**Delta**: (Bid - Ask) / Total
- Normalized measure of order flow direction
- Range: -1 (strong selling) to +1 (strong buying)

#### 2. Market Depth Analysis

**Support Levels (Bid Depth):**
- Previous swing lows with volume confirmation
- Acts as bid-heavy zones (buy orders cluster)
- Price likely to bounce when tested

**Resistance Levels (Ask Depth):**
- Previous swing highs with volume confirmation
- Acts as ask-heavy zones (sell orders cluster)
- Price likely to reject when tested

#### 3. Liquidity Concentration (Volume Nodes)

**High Volume Nodes:**
- Areas where price consolidated with above-average volume
- Indicates institutional accumulation/distribution
- Acts as magnets for price (attraction zones)

**Institutional Footprints:**
- High volume + narrow range = large orders working
- Multiple bars at same level = renewal of institutional interest
- Stronger than average liquidity nodes

#### 4. Absorption Detection

**Absorption Characteristics:**
- Volume increases significantly (>50% above average)
- Price range contracts (<80% of average)
- Indicates large limit order absorbing aggressive flow

**Buy Absorption:**
- Sellers aggressive but price can't go down
- Large bid limit orders absorbing supply
- Bullish signal (upside reversal likely)

**Sell Absorption:**
- Buyers aggressive but price can't go up
- Large ask limit orders absorbing demand
- Bearish signal (downside reversal likely)

#### 5. Order Flow Velocity

**Delta**: Current order flow direction (-1 to +1)
**Acceleration**: Change in delta (momentum of order flow)
**Momentum**: Average delta over window (persistent trend)
**Strength**: Consistency of direction (0 to 1)

#### 6. Exhaustion Detection

**Buying Exhaustion:**
- Price continues up but volume decreases
- Spread widening with low volume
- Running out of buyers above

**Selling Exhaustion:**
- Price continues down but volume decreases
- Spread widening with low volume
- Running out of sellers below
